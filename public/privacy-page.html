Privacy Policy.

What we collect. Why. And what we don't.

Overview

The Short Version

Late Culture is an editorial platform. We collect the minimum amount of information needed to operate this site and the Late Culture mobile app. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third-party advertisers.

This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over your information.

What We Collect

Information You Give Us

When you sign up for an account on the Late Culture app or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name (if you create an account)
  • Authentication data (if you sign in with Google)

When you use the Late Culture app, we may also collect venue check-ins, saves, and engagement data — only if you choose to use those features.

What We Collect Automatically

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use lateculture.com. This includes: page views, session duration, country, device type, and referral source. This data is anonymous and aggregated.

We do not use Google Analytics for advertising. We do not track you across other websites.

Cookies

What's in the Jar

Late Culture uses cookies for two purposes:

  • Essential cookies — required to keep you logged in and remember your preferences
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics, used to measure site usage

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. The site will still work, but some features may be limited.

Third Parties

Services We Use

Late Culture relies on a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — for user accounts and database
  • Sanity — for venue content management
  • Vercel — for hosting
  • Google Analytics — for traffic measurement
  • Google Maps — for venue locations

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We choose them based on their security practices and data handling standards.

Your Rights

What You Can Do

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Object to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hi@lateculture.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

Age Requirement

Late Culture is intended for users 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the date below and notify you via email if you have an account with us. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions

For any questions about this policy, your data, or how we operate, reach out to hi@lateculture.com.
